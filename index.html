Our Mission

To empower students with hands-on AI skills and real-world projects, preparing them for innovation in research, industry, and entrepreneurship.At NextGenAI, our mission is to foster a thriving ecosystem for artificial intelligence within our university. We are committed to advancing AI education, awareness, and innovation by empowering students with the knowledge, tools, and opportunities they need to excel. Through workshops, research projects, competitions, and collaborations, we strive to elevate the understanding and application of AI — ensuring the academic and professional growth of our members and contributing to the broader technological advancement of our university community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading force in shaping the future of artificial intelligence at our university by creating a collaborative, inclusive, and forward-thinking AI community. We aim to inspire innovation, bridge the gap between theory and real-world applications, and cultivate the next generation of AI leaders who will drive positive change in academia, industry, and society.
