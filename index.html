Our Mission

At NextGenAI, our mission is to foster a thriving ecosystem for artificial intelligence within our university. We are committed to advancing AI education, awareness, and innovation by empowering students with the knowledge, tools, and opportunities they need to excel.

Through workshops, research projects, competitions, and collaborations, we strive to elevate the understanding and application of AI — ensuring the academic and professional growth of our members and contributing to the broader technological advancement of our university community.
